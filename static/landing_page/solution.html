This isn’t a blank slate—it’s a compass pointing you towards better development habits. Every part of the tool, from ticket creation to sprint planning, comes with built-in recommendations, intelligent defaults, and automated next steps that reflect how great software is built.

  1. Opinionated Ticket Templates:

    Start every task on the right foot with structured, well-defined stories that focus on user value.

  2. Automated Prioritization:

    A workflow engine that doesn’t just track your tasks, but ranks them, ensuring you tackle the most impactful work first.

  3. AI-Assisted Ideation & Refinement:

    Generate, refine, and clarify requirements with an AI partner that understands coding best practices and product strategy.

  4. Guided Iterative Cycles:

    Built-in prompts and reminders to review, refine, and release frequently, capturing the essence of continuous delivery.
